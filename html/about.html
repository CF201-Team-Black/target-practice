Harper currently lives in Seattle with his wife, two boys (3 & 1 y/o), and 90lbs labradoodle. Originally from Uvalde, TX he grew up doing all the quintessential Texan activities like hunting, fishing, shooting competitively, and competing in the rodeo, as well as some “less Texan” activities such as playing tennis. After graduating high school, Harper attended the United States Naval Academy in Annapolis, MD and commissioned as a Special Operations Bomb Disposal Officer. In 2021, he transitioned from active duty into the private sector where he worked in Tech M&A advisory for Houlihan Lokey. He recently left his role as an investment banker to pursue software development and currently runs Rainier Racing Co., a Play-To-Earn Gaming NFT market analysis company.
